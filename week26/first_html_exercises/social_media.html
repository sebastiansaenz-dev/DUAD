Social Media

Social media refers to online platforms where users can share information and connect with virtual communities through text, video, photos, and other content. As of 2025, social media had more than five billion global users, which is equal to more than 68% of the world population.

Impact of Social Media

Social media has completely changed how we engage, communicate, and obtain information. It has several benefits, including interacting with loved ones, networking, and sharing content and ideas on a platform.
